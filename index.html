Welcome to GearUpForGood

GearUpForGood is a nonprofit organization founded by two high school students, dedicated to collecting and donating sports equipment to children in need. We aim to break down barriers to athletic participation by providing gear to kids who might otherwise miss out on the benefits of sports. We are located in Maryland and give all donations to other organizations, like LevelingThePlayingField.
