Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does GearUpForGood Do?

    We collect and redistribute new and gently used sports equipment to children who cannot afford it, helping them stay active and healthy.

  • Why is donating sports equipment important?

    Donating sports equipment reduces barriers to physical activity, combats medical disorders related to inactivity, and encourages a healthier lifestyle for kids in need.

  • How can I donate sports equipment?

    You can donate by bringing your items to one of our collection events or by contacting us directly to arrange a drop-off or pick-up.

  • What types of sports equipment do you accept?

    We accept a wide range of equipment, including balls, bats, gloves, cleats, helmets, and other sports-related items, as long as they are in wearable condition. No item is too small or too large!

  • Who benefits from these donations?

    The donations go to children from underserved communities who do not have access to sports equipment.

  • Can I donate used equipment?

    Yes, as long as the equipment is gently used, clean, and in good working condition, we are happy to accept it.

  • Do you partner with other organizations?

    Yes, much of our equipment goes to organizations like Leveling the Playing Field to ensure the equipment reaches children who need it most.

  • How can I volunteer?

    You can volunteer by helping at collection events or spreading awareness about our cause.

  • Are monetary donations accepted?

    Yes, monetary donations are accepted to buy new equipment for children in need. If you are wondering about making a donation, contact us!

Reach out if you have any more questions!
