Empowering Every Child to Play

Our Mission

We are committed to combating medical disorders related to lack of exercise by collecting and redistributing sports equipment to children who cannot afford it. We primarily collect equipment from other schools in the DMV. By providing access to sports gear, we help foster healthier lifestyles and encourage physical activity, enabling kids to improve their well-being through sports. Our goal is to ensure that no child is excluded from the positive impact that physical activity can have on their health and development.

Get Involved

There are many ways you can help us make a difference through donations. By donating new or gently used sports equipment, you directly support children who need it most, giving them the tools they need to stay active and healthy. Your generosity helps reduce the barriers to physical activity, empowering kids to improve their well-being through sports.
